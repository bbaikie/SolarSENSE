The Organic Farmer (TOF) - A magazine for African farmers

The Organic Farmer (TOF) is a magazine with practical information, it is published monthly and is distributed free of charge to interested farmer groups in Kenya. 60,000 farmers already receive concrete guidance and practical tips on securing and increasing their harvests through simple, environmentally friendly means.
