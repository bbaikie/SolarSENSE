WEEDING AND CROP MANAGEMENT
Early weeding is very important for the successful establishment
of a Push-pull plot. We carried out the first weeding when maize
was 3 weeks old, and second weeding when maize was 5 weeks old.
It is important to distinguish between desmodium and weeds.
